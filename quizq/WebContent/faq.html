Frequently Asked Questions

What is the quiz for?

Professors can evaluate the students on the learnings as well as parents can evaluate their children on different learnings.

What are the type of questions I can expect?

Questions mainly consist of topics from C , HTML , JAVA, Python.

Who can take the quiz?

Anyone with the intent to assess their skills.

Can professor see my marks?

Yes there is provision made for the professor to assess their students by seeing their marks.

Is it a non-profit organization?

Yes it is a non-profit organization open to all.
